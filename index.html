Favoriting made simple and private!

Dock any listed item directly to the browser screen with full privacy and reduce up and down scroll time on any endless scrolling website. Its a simple way to favorite a item listed on any gallery(or grid or tile) view screens in websites like youtube feed, google image search, pinterest pins, amazon product search, giphy, and many other websites. This tool functions very much like an webpage internal bookmark tool, but instead of saving the URL links, it stores the partial html content in your browser's local storage. Try this on your favorite website today.
